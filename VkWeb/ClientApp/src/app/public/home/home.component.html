Our Vocabulary Recipe

A complete solution for teaching vocabulary in context.

1. Engage
Pick something interesting for your class to read. The world is your textbook.
2. Level
Use the profiler to adjust it to the appropriate level.
3. Define
Use the built-in dictionaries or add your own definitions or translations to new words.
4. Share
Organize readings and share them with students using tags.
5. Pretest
Before each reading, VocabKitchen pretests students so they only spend time on words they don't know.
6. Read
Students can view contextualized definitions as they read.
7. Learn
VocabKitchen generates individualized, scaffolded vocabulary practice that combine pronunciation, spelling and meaning.
8. Review
Use data about each student's progress to guide review activities.
