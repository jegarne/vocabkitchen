Welcome to the new VocabKitchen!

We're currently testing our redesign. Documentation will be limited as we finalize the site over the next 6 months, so be brave and click around.

I'll be testing the application in the St. Louis, Missouri area this spring, but I'd like to hear from great language teachers all over the world.

When you sign up here you'll be the administrator of your organization (for example, your school) and you'll be able to invite as many teachers as you'd like. My recommendation is to try it out with one other teacher in your school. For now you can only invite 60 students. If you'd like to add more than 60 students to your organization, contact me at contact@vocabkitchen.com.

I will be testing and making changes to the site through mid-year 2020. Some features may change and I can't guarantee that your content will remain available or that everything will work smoothly. My goal is to have a stable and well-tested site in time for language programs starting in August, 2020.

If you were a user of the old version of the site, you'll need to sign up for a new account. There are currently no plans to migrate data or accounts from the old sites.
